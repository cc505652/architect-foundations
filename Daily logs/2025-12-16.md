## What I learned

- Linux filesystem structure
- Package management
- Basic networking commands
- OSI model with real mappings

## What could go wrong?

- Misconfigured files in /etc can break systems
- Unpatched systems are vulnerable
- Network traffic can be intercepted at multiple layers

## How would I defend it?

- Principle of least privilege
- Regular updates
- Firewalls and segmentation

## Tradeoffs

- Security vs convenience
- Simplicity vs flexibility
